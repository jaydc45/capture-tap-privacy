Privacy Policy for Capture Tap

Last updated: July 1, 2025

Quick Summary: Capture Tap is a simple action game that uses ads to stay free. We collect minimal data and respect your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Game Data:

  • Game progress and high scores (stored locally on your device)
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Level completion and star ratings

Advertising Data:

  • Device advertising identifier (for personalized ads)
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage patterns for ad frequency

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Game Experience: Save your progress and provide personalized gameplay
  • Advertising: Show relevant ads to keep the game free
  • App Improvement: Understand how players use the game

3. Advertising and Third Parties

Capture Tap uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Data Usage

4. Your Choices and Controls

  • Ad Tracking: You can limit ad tracking in your iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Personalized Ads: Disable in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
  • Remove Ads: Purchase the ad-free version within the app
  • Reset Data: Delete and reinstall the app to clear all local data

5. Data Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store personal information on our servers. Ad-related data is handled securely by Google AdMob according to their privacy standards.

6. Children's Privacy

Capture Tap is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game only collects anonymous usage data for advertising purposes.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Data Retention

Game progress data is retained until you delete the app. Advertising data is managed by Google AdMob according to their retention policies.
